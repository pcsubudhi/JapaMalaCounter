Words per mantra chant: ?

"Hare Krishna Hare Krishna Krishna Krishna Hare Hare" = 8 words. "Om Namah Shivaya" = 3 words. "Om" = 1 word. Count the words in one full round of your mantra.

Chants per Mala: ?

Traditionally a japa mala has 108 beads. Each bead = 1 full chant of your mantra. Some practitioners use 54 (half mala) or 216 (double). Choose based on your mala or preference.

ISKCON recommends minimum 16 rounds (malas) daily for initiated devotees. Start with what's comfortable for you — even 1 or 4 rounds is a great beginning! 🙏
